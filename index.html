About

Hi, I'm Chris. I'm a front end web developer who has developed a passion for coding and technology in general.

My philosophy for writing code is to make the complex simple and communicate well. It is one thing to write code, another to make it easy for others to follow, and a whole different thing entirely to communicate clearly what you are trying to do.

I have an interest in breaking apart large codebases to reverse engineer how things work, constantly questioning if things can be done in a better way.

Check out my links and don't hesitate to get into contact.
